About Flora Art Casa

Where centuries of Foshan ceramic tradition meets contemporary design excellence

Our Story

Flora Art Casa, formally known as Foshan Mosaic & Art Tile Co., was founded in the heart of China's ceramics capital — Foshan. For over five years, we have been dedicated to the development, production, and sales of a wide range of mosaics and art tiles.

Our mission is to bring creativity and quality into every space through meticulously crafted products. We serve wholesalers, importers, and large-scale projects around the world, delivering tiles that transform ordinary spaces into extraordinary experiences.

Our Vision

To be the leading provider of mosaic and art tiles worldwide, recognized for our innovation, craftsmanship, and commitment to sustainability.

Every tile we produce reflects our core values: innovation in design, precision in manufacturing, and integrity in every business relationship. Our state-of-the-art facility in Foshan combines advanced technology with artisan craftsmanship.

Factory Capabilities

Our modern production facility spans thousands of square meters, equipped with cutting-edge machinery for ceramic, porcelain, glass, and natural stone tile manufacturing.

We maintain strict quality control at every stage — from raw material selection to final packaging. Our R10-rated anti-slip pool tiles meet the highest international safety standards, giving architects and designers confidence in specifying our products for any project.
